— About Us —

Born in Sanghar,
Loved by All

O-Bailia Restaurant is Sanghar's premier fast-casual dining destination. Nestled on Nawabshah Road, we craft bold flavours through our legendary Lava Burgers, hand-stretched Crown Crust Pizzas, and hearty continental spreads — all prepared with the freshest local ingredients and an unyielding passion for taste.

Our kitchen never sleeps. Whether you crave a midnight Broast or a spicy Hot & Sour Soup on a winter evening, O-Bailia delivers warmth, taste, and speed — straight to your door via WhatsApp ordering.
